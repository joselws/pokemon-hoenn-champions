# Pokemon Hoenn Champions

This hackrom aims to offer the same experience as the original Pokemon Emerald game, but with all Pokemon from the Hoenn Pokedex available, quality-of-life improvements, rebalancing, and increased challenge:

- All opponent battles are harder (gym leaders, trainers, rival encounters, magma/aqua battles, elite 4)
- All Pokemon from Sapphire, Ruby, and Emerald available at their respective locations. For example, you may now catch a Zangoose or a Roselia in the locations they intended to be.
- Quality of life improvements include:
    - Being able to purchase stones and TMs at the battle frontier and Ever Grande city.
    - Battle frontier losses don't make you lose all your points, instead, you get placed to the previous tier.
    - Miracle island is not as hard to find now.
    - No transfer or link table needed to evolve certain Pokemon
    - Clamperl items to evolve now function as evolutionary stones, and are available for purchase at the battle frontier.
    - You are allowed to use all pokemon without restrictions in the battle frontier.
- Rebalancing includes:
    - Some battle moves with change in power, PP, effect, or probability of triggering such effect.
    - Some Pokemon stats changed
    - Some Pokemon learnsets changed
    - Some Pokemon experience type changed
    - Some Pokemon level pre-requisite for evolution changed
- Ghost type is now considered a special type
- Dark type is now considered a physical type

## Stats rework

Some Pokemon stats have been improved to encourage players to use them in their playthrough. There were no heavy changes to make Pokemon lose their identity, but to make them more viable for players who like to use them. The philosophy is: Good pokemon are still as good, and bad pokemon are now viable. For example, Spinda still sucks but now not as much.
